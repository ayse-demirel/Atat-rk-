MUSTAFA KEMAL ATATÜRK

You became the leader not only of the Turkish nation, but also of the oppressed nations in the world.

Here's a time line of Atatürk's life:

  • 1881 - Born in Selanik,Greece
  • 1905 January 11 - He graduated from the Military Academy as a Captain and was assigned to Damascus to do his internship in the 30th Cavalry Regiment of the 5th Army.
  • 1906 October - He founded the Vatan ve Hürriyet Cemiyeti in Damascus. He did his artillery internship in Damascus and became Kolağası.
  • 1909 March 31 - He worked as a Staff Officer of the Movement Army during the March 31 revolution.
  • 1911 September 13 -Mustafa Kemal was transferred to Istanbul to the General Staff.
  • 1911 November 27 - Mustafa Kemal was promoted to Major.
  • 1913 October 27: - Mustafa Kemal appointed to Sofia Attaché
  • 1914 March 1 - Mustafa Kemal promoted to Lieutenant Colonel
  • 1915 February 2 - Mustafa Kemal established the 19th Division in Tekirdagi.
  • 1915 June 1 - Mustafa Kemal was promoted to the rank of Colonel..
  • 1915 August 9: -Mustafa Kemal was appointed to the Anafartalar Group Command.
  • 1916 April 1 - Mustafa Kemal was promoted to Brigadier General.
  • 1919 June 15 - Mustafa Kemal received the title of 3rd Army Inspector.
  • 1919 July 8 / 9: - Mustafa Kemal withdrew from the military. (Time: 20:50)
  • 1920 April 23 - Mustafa Kemal opened the Turkish Grand National Assembly in Ankara
  • 1920 April 24 -Mustafa Kemal was elected President of the Grand National Assembly
  • 1920 January 9 / 10 -I. İnönü War
  • 1921 March 30 / Nisan 1 -II.İnönü War
  • 1921 August 5: -Mustafa Kemal was given the post of Commander-in-Chief
  • 1921 September 13 -Under the direction of Mustafa Kemal, the Battle of Sakarya was won.
  • 1921 September 19 -Mustafa Kemal was given the rank of Marshal and was given the title of Mustafa Kemal Gazi
  • 1923 October 29 -Republic was proclaimed. -Gazi Mustafa Kemal became the first President
  • 1935 March 1: -Atatürk was elected President for the 4th time.
  • 1938 September 15 -Atatürk wrote his will.
  • 1938 November 10 -Ataturk is dead. (Thursday, at 09.05)
